TO OUR FRONTLINERS

The video features the sisters of Theta Phi Alpha from The College of New Jersey thanking essential workers for their hard work. The organization’s chaplain and I wrote the script, and each clip was recorded by the speaker. I edited the video using Premiere Pro. The video is posted on the organization’s official YouTube page and shared on Instagram and Facebook.

THE IDEA

The idea of making a thank you video emerged when one of the members of the organization shared how her mother, a nurse, had become very overwhelmed by the conditions of her work environment. In response, the organization wanted to create a thank you video expressing their gratitude to her and all essential workers.

Script

On behalf of the sisters of Theta Phi Alpha, the Beta Beta chapter, we wanted to extend our love to you.
To the nurses, working endless hours to ensure our safety, thank you.
To the doctors, working tirelessly to heal us and find a cure, thank you.
To the food service employees, working early mornings stocking and delivering our food, thank you.
To the firefighters, responding to calls and still spreading joy through holiday runs and birthday parades, thank you.
To the EMT’s, being the first to provide medical service and responding to your pagers at all hours, thank you.
To the police officers, keeping our towns safe and your residents smiling, thank you.
To the public transport operators, for protecting their passengers and guiding them to their destinations, thank you.
To the teachers, bringing their classrooms into their homes and planning for their students from afar, thank you.
To the families, making memories at home and doing your part in slowing the spread, thank you.
To all our essential workers, thank you for your daily dedication and sacrifice.
We are in awe of your courage.
As you continue to fight this battle, we will continue to keep you in our thoughts and prayers,
and appreciate your skills and your sacrifice.
From the bottom of our hearts,
thank you.
