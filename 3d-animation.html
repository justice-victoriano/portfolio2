3D ANIMATION

The 10 second animation features a fish discovering a phone in the water and becomes startled when it vibrates. The modeling and animation were done entirely in Maya.

THE PROCESS

I used a picture of a copperband butterfly fish to act as my reference as I modeled my fish. After the model was completed, I UV mapped my fish and created and baked a projection map to add color. To add texture, I used Photoshop to generate a black and white version of my image for my bump map. I used a sine handle to animate the wriggling of my fishâ€™s body, and created a path for the fish to travel on. I used Arnold for lighting and rendered via playblast. I animated at 24 frames per second.
