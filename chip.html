CHIP LOGO

The Community Health Initiative of Philadelphia is a student-run organization at the University of Pennsylvania that focuses on public health within the city of Philadelphia. I created a logo that features the Liberty Bell, with hands in the negative space, symbolizing outreach.

THE PROCESS

When the organization first approached me, they already had the idea of the logo resembling the famous Love Sculpture displayed on the campus of the University of Pennsylvania. However, the idea was later dropped and I was given free reign to design the logo. When creating the logo, I wanted to represent the true purpose of the organization by incorporating the aspect of outreach as well as an element that represents the city. After much iteration, the design of the Liberty Bell with the hands in the negative space was chosen.
