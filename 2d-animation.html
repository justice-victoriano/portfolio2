2D ANIMATION

I am in the process of creating an animation about an impoverished family in the Philippines. The story is centered around two daughters and their journey to find their father a birthday present. Each frame is entirely hand-drawn using Procreate, and the clips were compiled using Premiere Pro. I animated at 6 frames per second. The video shows a draft of the first thirty seconds of the story.

THE IDEA

I started this project because I wanted to challenge myself to learn frame-by-frame animation and see what the outcome would be from this experience. I am proficient in Maya but prior to this project, I have never had any experience with traditional animation. I wanted to create a heart-warming story that shows a glimpse of the lives and struggles that many impoverished people from the Philippines, my home country, face.
