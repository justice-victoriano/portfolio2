LUCKY CHARMS REDESIGN

When creating the package, I wanted to evoke a sense of playfulness and light-heartedness that the Lucky Charms cereal brand is known for. I had the idea of incorporating elements of the existing theme into the actual shape of the cereal box. I redesigned the box to be a pot of gold with a retractable rainbow spout dispenser. THE PROCESS

I created the templates and design on Adobe Illustrator. To assemble the package, I printed the designs on 11x17 paper and used Artist Tac to paste each individual piece on Bristol Paper, which would make the packaging sturdier. After cutting the pieces out, I hot glued them together.
