Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Justice Victoriano and I am a senior majoring in Interactive Multimedia, with minors in Graphic Design and Marketing at The College of New Jersey. I specialize in animation, video-editing, and design, and am proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Maya, HTML, and CSS.

Like many artists, I am not alone in saying that I have always had a passion for art—how could I not? The possibilities for self expression are limitless, and the ability to imagine something and then create it is unparalleled compared to other fields. Growing up, my favorite mediums were graphite and acrylic, but in college, I discovered the various ways that art can be expressed through technology. This opened up a whole new world for me and soon enough, I became a believer in the power of digital art. This portfolio serves as the manifestation of my growth as a creative and evidence of my artistic passion. Every work I present here is a labor of love.
