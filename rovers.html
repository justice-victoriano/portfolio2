ROVERS

The animation was inspired by Curiosity, the Mars Rover who sings "Happy Birthday" to itself on the anniversary of its landing. Rovers functions as a backstory for this real life event.

YEAR

2020

SOFTWARES USED

After Effects
Photoshop

THE PROCESS

All the assets were drawn in Photoshop and were then brought into After Effects where they were arranged in a 3D space. I wanted the animation to have a storybook feel, which was why it was written in third person. The narration was done by Megan Rulon.

Assets were drawn in Photoshop.

Assets were made into a 3D Layers.

Characters were animated using the puppet tool.
