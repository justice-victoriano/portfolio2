IF I COULD GET OUT OF THIS PLACE

I was inspired by Billy Joel's "Piano Man," which is a song about hopeless and unfulfilled people who congregate at a bar and request songs from the piano man to temporarily relieve them of their burdens. This animation features a character inspired by the song: a hopeless woman longing to escape her reality. Her desire to break free from her situation is represented in the smoke of the cigarette which transforms into symbols of freedom.

THE PROCESS

The hand, woman, and dandelions are all hand-drawn in Photoshop while the rest of the assets are made in After Effects. The song in the animation is the instrumental version of Joji's "Slow Dancing in the Dark." I chose this song because it captures the dream-like feeling that I wanted to convey in the animation.
